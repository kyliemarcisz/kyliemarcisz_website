🔊 the science

Spatial Audio Engineering

3D spatial audio tricks your brain into hearing sound from anywhere in space — above, behind, across the room — through just two speakers or headphones. I've spent the last three years building systems that do exactly this.

🔊
BACCH Laboratories
Cross-talk Cancellation · Personal Sound Zone

Working with Dr. Edgar Choueiri's technology, I prototype and tune 8-speaker Personal Sound Zone arrays that create truly immersive 3D audio without headphones — using a proprietary cross-talk cancellation algorithm to deliver different sound to each ear.

8-speaker array I built in my dorm room, Fall 2025 — presented at CES 2026
Array Beamforming EQ Optimization CES 2023–2026
🔬
Princeton 3D3A Lab
AI Head-Tracking · Anechoic Research

Selected as 1 of 4 students from 400+ applicants for the Princeton Laboratory Learning Program. I worked alongside Prof. Edgar Choueiri evaluating AI-integrated head-tracking algorithms inside an anechoic chamber — and integrated those findings directly into BACCH's Optimal Room Correction prototype.

Key insight: Real-time head-tracking dramatically improves the perceived stability of a virtual sound source — the brain expects audio to stay fixed in space even as you move.
